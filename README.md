Go-Mission & Vision
--------------------------------------
Go has absolutely a different mission and vision than other traditional location-tracking applications. Have you ever visited wonderful places or passed by while driving -
wished to know the addresses of those locations at go time? Through Go's intuitive design and user-friendly layout -pinning/tracking those moments are more accessible than ever! 
While using Go, you will be able to create journals of your locations at run time, sharing any of those with your friends and family, and importantly -while doing so, you will 
have complete control of your data and privacy.

Go is a lightweight application; developed with device-centric design and performance in mind. Soon, it plans to add some unique features like taking small notes in audio and text 
format and sharing journals with your friends from other APIs (such as Meta, Instagram, and Twitter) with permission. We strongly believe by using Go, you will feel its intention 
and power, and with your acceptance, Go will be a part of your daily gadget while traveling the whole world!
